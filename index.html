Our mission is to bridge the gap between India and the global design landscape, offering the latest in bathroom, ceramic, and wellness products. We aim to make the selection process seamless, inspiring clients to connect with their personal spaces and infuse them with beauty and functionality.

A Tradition Of Excellence

Elysium's story began in 1979 when the founder's family opened its first store in Kolkata. Over decades of commitments and efforts, the business expanded to five showrooms in Kolkata, with offices in Delhi and Gujarat. Elysium is the culmination of this rich legacy, continuing the tradition dedication to of superior craftsmanship and growth.
